ABOUT US

Our Company Bike sale was founded by Shop Express Inc. in 2010 and is committed to give you a smart shopping experience at great prices. We believe that everyone deserves the best prices and it is a privilege for us to make goods accessible and affordable for you.

Our Gaurantee

Bike sale company ships all orders on the same day and all goods are subject to quality checks. If you encounter any issue on the delivered goods, it would be our pleasure to issue a refund right away. All goods are on a money-back guarantee.

History

Bike sale first opened in 2010 at Westwood in Malton Missisaugua ON, Canada with the goal of offering the best bikes with great options. We appreciate the trust you give us through your purchases and we strive to give you the most value for your hard-earned money.

Today, Bike Sale Company has 100 stores in Canada. We pride ourselves in our great customer service and we look forward to seeing you in our stores or getting your orders online.
